OUR STORY

WHERE LOVE TURNS
INTO MIRACLES

There are dreams woven through glances and smiles… And there are days when love becomes whole – a wedding day.

Celestial Dream was born from hearts full of wonder people who believe that love deserves to be celebrated in its most beautiful, purest form.

With every petal every light, every song we craft not just a day but a feeling you’ll carry with you for a lifetime.

And we are the quiet artists behind the scenes carefully crafting every detail so that on your day, all you need to do is listen to your heart. Every couple is a love song and every wedding a stage set for emotion.

We believe every wedding should feel like a beautiful memory one that stays in your heart forever just like your love. We don’t just organize weddings we tell your love story through flowers, light, music, and the softest of emotions.

From golden sunsets on the beach to sundrenched gardens in summer or candlelit halls like fairy tales we bring your dreams to life.

Because love is the most beautiful thing in the world. And a wedding… is the very first miracle of happiness. When you look back, you won’t just remember how it looked but how it felt to stand there, hand in hand, wrapped in a dream made just for two.

“TOGETHER, FOREVER.”

“HAND IN HAND, ALL LIFE LONG.”

“YOU AND I, THAT’S ENOUGH.”
